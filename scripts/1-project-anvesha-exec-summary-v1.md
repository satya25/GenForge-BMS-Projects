# Project Anvesha – Executive Summary

## 1. Vision & Purpose
Anvesha is designed as the institute’s analytics and insights engine. Its purpose is to consolidate exam performance data, question usage statistics, and blueprint compliance into actionable dashboards. It empowers faculty and administrators to make data-driven decisions about teaching effectiveness, question bank health, and exam fairness.

## 2. Objectives
- Provide real-time analytics on exam papers and student performance.
- Track question usage, difficulty distribution, and Bloom’s taxonomy coverage.
- Enable faculty to identify gaps in syllabus coverage and course outcome alignment.
- Support administrators with institute-wide comparative analytics.
- Integrate seamlessly with Viveka (evaluation system) and Yojanā (paper generation).

## 3. Scope
**In-Scope:**
- Faculty dashboards with subject-level analytics.
- Question bank usage reports and growth tracking.
- Blueprint compliance monitoring.
- Integration with Viveka and Yojanā APIs.

**Out-of-Scope:**
- Direct exam paper generation (handled by Yojanā).
- Student-facing portals.
- Predictive AI models for question creation (future phase).

## 4. Stakeholders
- **Faculty:** monitor question usage, blueprint compliance, and student performance.
- **HODs:** oversee department-level analytics and ensure syllabus coverage.
- **Exam Coordinators:** validate paper fairness and statistical equivalence.
- **Administrators:** track institute-wide adoption and compliance.
- **Academic Council:** review analytics for policy and accreditation.

## 5. Key Features
- Interactive dashboards with drill-down capabilities.
- Statistical equivalence testing of paper variants.
- Automated compliance checks for Bloom’s levels and CO mappings.
- Growth tracking of question banks across semesters.
- Exportable reports for accreditation and audits.

## 6. Success Criteria
- ≥90% faculty adoption within the first semester.
- Accurate compliance reporting with <5% deviation.
- 20% increase in question bank growth per semester.
- Demonstrated time savings in exam review processes.
- Zero discrepancies in audit reports.

## 7. Roadmap Snapshot
- **MVP:** Basic logs and faculty dashboards.
- **Phase I:** Integration with Viveka and Yojanā APIs.
- **Phase II:** Predictive insights on question performance.
- **Phase III:** Institute-wide comparative analytics and ERP integration.
