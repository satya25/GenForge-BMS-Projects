# Project Samaya – Executive Summary

## 1. Vision & Purpose
Samaya is envisioned as the institute’s intelligent time and schedule management system. Its purpose is to streamline academic planning, exam scheduling, and resource allocation. By integrating with other GenAI systems, Samaya ensures that faculty, students, and administrators have a unified view of academic timelines and deadlines.

## 2. Objectives
- Provide centralized scheduling for exams, classes, and academic events.
- Enable faculty to manage deadlines for blueprint creation and paper generation.
- Offer students clear visibility into exam schedules and preparation timelines.
- Support administrators with institute-wide calendar management.
- Integrate seamlessly with Sangrah (data consolidation) and Yojanā (paper generation).

## 3. Scope
**In-Scope:**
- Academic calendar management.
- Exam scheduling and notifications.
- Faculty deadline tracking for paper generation.
- Integration with Viveka and Anvesha for exam-related data.

**Out-of-Scope:**
- Direct paper generation (handled by Yojanā).
- Student performance analytics (handled by Anvesha).
- Non-academic event management (future phase).

## 4. Stakeholders
- **Faculty:** manage deadlines for blueprint creation and paper generation.
- **Students:** access exam schedules and preparation timelines.
- **HODs:** oversee department-level scheduling and compliance.
- **Exam Coordinators:** manage exam timetables and logistics.
- **Administrators:** maintain institute-wide academic calendar.

## 5. Key Features
- Centralized academic calendar with role-based views.
- Automated reminders for faculty deadlines.
- Exam scheduling with conflict detection.
- Integration with Yojanā for paper readiness tracking.
- Exportable schedules for students and faculty.

## 6. Success Criteria
- ≥90% accuracy in scheduling with zero conflicts.
- ≥80% faculty adoption for deadline tracking.
- Improved student preparedness (≥15% increase in on-time submissions).
- Seamless integration with Yojanā and Anvesha.
- Positive feedback from exam coordinators (≥8/10 satisfaction).

## 7. Roadmap Snapshot
- **MVP:** Basic academic calendar and exam scheduling.
- **Phase I:** Faculty deadline tracking and reminders.
- **Phase II:** Integration with Yojanā and Anvesha.
- **Phase III:** Predictive scheduling and ERP integration.
