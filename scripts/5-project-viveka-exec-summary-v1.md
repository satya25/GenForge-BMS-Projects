# Project Viveka – Executive Summary

## 1. Vision & Purpose
Viveka is envisioned as the institute’s evaluation and grading system. Its purpose is to provide secure, consistent, and transparent evaluation of exam papers. By integrating with Yojanā (paper generation) and Sangrah (archival), Viveka ensures that student performance is assessed fairly, efficiently, and in alignment with course outcomes.

## 2. Objectives
- Automate evaluation workflows for faculty and examiners.
- Ensure consistency in grading across multiple evaluators.
- Provide secure access to evaluated papers and results.
- Track performance against Bloom’s taxonomy and course outcomes.
- Integrate with Anvesha for analytics and Samaya for scheduling.

## 3. Scope
**In-Scope:**
- Faculty evaluation dashboards.
- Automated grading support for objective questions.
- Secure storage and retrieval of evaluated papers.
- Integration with Yojanā and Sangrah repositories.

**Out-of-Scope:**
- Paper generation (handled by Yojanā).
- Accreditation reporting (handled by Anvesha).
- Student-facing portals for practice (handled by Prabodha).

## 4. Stakeholders
- **Faculty:** evaluate papers, track grading consistency, and access dashboards.
- **Exam Coordinators:** oversee evaluation workflows and timelines.
- **HODs:** monitor department-level grading and compliance.
- **Administrators:** ensure secure storage and retrieval of evaluated papers.
- **Academic Council:** review evaluation consistency for accreditation.

## 5. Key Features
- Automated grading for objective-type questions.
- Faculty dashboards with grading consistency metrics.
- Secure storage of evaluated papers with audit logs.
- Integration with Yojanā (paper generation) and Sangrah (archival).
- Exportable evaluation reports for accreditation and audits.

## 6. Success Criteria
- ≥95% accuracy in automated grading for objective questions.
- ≥90% faculty adoption of evaluation dashboards.
- Zero discrepancies in grading audits.
- Seamless integration with Yojanā, Sangrah, and Anvesha.
- Positive feedback from exam coordinators (≥8/10 satisfaction).

## 7. Roadmap Snapshot
- **MVP:** Automated grading for objective questions.
- **Phase I:** Faculty dashboards and grading consistency metrics.
- **Phase II:** Integration with Yojanā and Sangrah.
- **Phase III:** Predictive analytics on student performance and ERP integration.
